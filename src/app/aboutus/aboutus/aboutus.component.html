Introduction

Enco Engineers Combine Pvt. Ltd. established in 1983 are leading manufacturers of high precision Sheet Metal Parts and Assemblies for Automobile, White Goods and Engineering Industries. ENCO is a leading sheet metal and suspension component supplier to to Maruti Suzuki.

Our manufacturing facilities are located in Gurgaon, Dharuhera and Chennai.

Quality

  • We were among the first SMEs in India to get ISO 9001 certification in 1994. Our directors were awarded by prime minister Pamulaparti Venkata Narasimha Rao for this achievement.
  • ISO/TS 16949: is a quality management system that applies to design, development, production, quality, continuous improvement and supply chain. We switched to ISO/TS 16949 in 2005 as the system was more applicable to our processes.
  • OHSAS 18001: is a British government standard for occupational health and safety management systems. We have been OHSAS 18001 certified since 2012.
  • ISO 14001: is a standard for reducing the negative environmental footprint of our operations. We have been ISO 14001 certified since 2012.
