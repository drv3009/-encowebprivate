Mechanical Presses

We use only the highest quality pneumatic clutch presses from reputed manufacturers globally. We only use new machines as in our experience these deliver the best performance for our customers. Currently we are able to offer pressing loads up 600 Tons.

In some cases hydraulic pressing is required by customers for specialized parts. Our engineering teams will be happy to get involved to decide which products would be better manufactured with hydraulic lines.
