Tubular Processing

We have been manufacturing tubular parts with operations such as swaging, pipe cutting, chamfering, tubular bending, tubular welding, bushes from pipes as well as sheets, seam welded short tubes or bushes and many other parts since 1987. We are dealing with pipes with thickness from 1mm to 4mm and diameter of 20mm to 70mm. We are very interested in serving your tubular parts requirements today.
