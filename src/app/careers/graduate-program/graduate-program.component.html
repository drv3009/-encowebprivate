University Students

Our graduate trainee program (GTP) has built a strong platform for many in the industry. It is a 2 year program with 6 month or yearly rotations through multiple functions. At the end of the program you get the opportunity to interview again with the teams you like the most. If you are a college student interested in this program please write to us at hr@enco.in with “GTP:” in the subject. Students with graduate, post graduate or doctorate degrees may apply.
